Derrick Gichaba

        I am a multidisciplinary artist, born in Kenya and based in Minnesota,

    who has found passion for dance, photography, film, and fashion!



    As a kid, I always found myself dancing for fun with my family and friends,

    but I've been researching and teaching myself to be a professional dancer since 2014.

    Growing up in an African American culture, I picked up Breakdancing for my

    style of dance and eventually ventured off into Hip Hop freestyling.


                    
    During my journey through high school, I had the privilege of connecting

    with others who also enjoyed dancing! We spent a lot of time creating

    spaces together to express ourselves through movement. As time moved on, 

    we continued developing our skills as dancers,and eventually became

    colleagues by creating a crew known as Squad 7.


                    
    This community of dancers has connected me with a monumental amount of artists,

    not only within the Twin Cities,but across the nation and overseas! Ironically,

    this led me to pursue photography and videography professionally.At the age of 16,

    I was able to save up money in high school to buy my first camera—that is when the

    journey of this art discipline began. Seeing different movement artists expressing

    themselves from a plethora of backgrounds gave me the desire to capture art digitally. 


                    
    So far in my career as a dancer, I became a member of a dance crew that trains and

    performs together. I also teach Hip Hop at an up and coming dance studio called

    Hothouse Mpls, and I host sessions where people of all dance backgrounds can customized

    learn and express themselves! 


                    
    In regards to my career as a photographer and videographer, I’ve had the pleasure of

    working with multiple local artists of all disciplines, while also taking advantage of

    the opportunity to shoot big name artists like: Ari Lennox, Noname, Mereba to name a few. 


                    
    During the pandemic, I picked up the art of fashion styling and design. I’ve been successful

    in creating customized clothing and designing items of my own. I’m in the process of creating

    a brand where I’ll be able to create clothing for myself and for others to enjoy!
